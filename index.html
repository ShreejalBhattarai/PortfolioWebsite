My name is Shreejal Bhattarai. I am a Junior at Texas State University and have developed a keen passion towards computers, logic, and electronics. I am interested in Microprocessors, Digital Design, Analog Design, Microcontrollers, Embedded and Quantum Electronics.

Currently, I am:

  • Continuously expanding my programming skills, picking up new tools and languages that help me bridge the gap between hardware and software,
  • Seeking genuine connections with people who care about building something meaningful, and hoping, in some small way, to leave a positive mark on the lives I cross paths with.
